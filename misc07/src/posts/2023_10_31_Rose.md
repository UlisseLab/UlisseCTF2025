## October 31th, 2023, the Stone Doesn’t Bloom For Everyone
 – In the Place Between Breath and Ash

They talk about the Rose of Stone as if it were an object. A prize.
But it’s not something you hold in your hand.
It’s something that presses back from the inside —
and not everyone survives the weight.

The Rose doesn’t open for the clever.
It doesn’t bloom for the fast.

It reveals itself only to those who have passed through the fire.
Through the black.
Through the tearing.

Nigredo — when all certainties rot.
Albedo — when the mind sees but doesn’t yet understand.
Citrinitas — when symbols burn brighter than facts.
Rubedo — when opposites dissolve, and the Self stands alone, whole, and shaking.

Only then, at the end, does the Rose unfold.
Not in glory, not in gold —
but in silence.

If you’re looking for the flag, you’ve missed it.
If you’re chasing victory, she will hide.
But if you’ve stood in the dark long enough
to let it speak,
and listened until you stopped needing answers—

Then you’re close.

— L.C.
