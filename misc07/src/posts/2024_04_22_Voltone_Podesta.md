## April 22nd, Voltone del Podestà

![Voltone](/static/images/voltone-del-podestà.jpg.webp)

I stood in the archway for too long today.
You can hear people speak —
but not see them.
Their voices travel without bodies,
bounce off stone like ghosts looking for someone to haunt.

And then there was something else.

Not a voice.

A melody.

Like an old song trying to remember itself.

It circled the vault twice,
and I swear — just for a second —
it wasn’t in the air anymore.
It was in my chest.


I think the echo knows more than it should.
Or maybe it remembers where it came from.

And if that’s true…
then sound isn’t the only thing that travels sideways in this city.

— L.C.
