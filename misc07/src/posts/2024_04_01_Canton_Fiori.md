## April 1st – Canton dei Fiori

April 23rd – Canton dei Fiori
![](/static/images/cannabis-protectio.jpg)
> Panis vita. 
> Canabis protectio. 
> Vinum laetitia.

A motto carved for those who needed to live well.
Bread, safety, joy. No city can grow without them.
But this isn’t all Bologna was built on.
For centuries, people came here not for trade, but for words.They crossed borders to sit in crowded rooms,to listen, to argue, to understand.

**They called it a Studium. It shaped minds, laws, and the way people saw the world.** It wasn’t marked on the walls, but in books, in speech, in memory.

This part of the city doesn’t speak as loudly. **But it’s still here. If you know how to listen.**

— L.C.
