## March 21st – Corte Isolani

![CorteIsolani](/static/images/bologna-corte-isolani.jpg)
![Freccia](/static/images/freccia.jpg)

Three arrows are still here. Pinned like afterthoughts into wood, stuck mid-air as if they never meant to land.

But no one talks about the fourth.
Not because it’s lost — but because it finished what the others couldn’t.

I stood beneath the beam today. Closed my eyes. Imagined its flight.

It didn’t aim for flesh. Didn’t care for vengeance. It followed a different path. **Southwest**. Through the cloisters, past the empty chapels,cutting across time like a needle.

**If you trace the angle, if you walk it with patience, you’ll find a place with no arrows left — just wounds shaped like questions.**

That’s where it landed. And if the wood still remembers the sound of it leaving, the stone remembers the sound of it arriving.

— L.C.
