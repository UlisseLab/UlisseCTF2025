## March 5th – Basilica Santo Stefano

![SantoStefano](/static/images/santo-stefano.jpg)

They call it the Complex of Seven Churches.
But there aren’t seven anymore.
Or maybe there never were.

Some were demolished.
Some collapsed.
Some were never there to begin with —
except in someone’s head.


The air inside shifts from room to room, like memory changing its mind.

One chapel feels Roman.
Another one feels Eastern.
One smells like dust.
Another like blood.
And in one, I could swear the light was coming from the floor.

People talk about the Passion of Christ, the sepulchre, the sacred geometry.
I see bones. I see repetition.
I see a city trying to stitch time together
with bricks that don’t match.

They say Saint Petronius built it to mirror Rome.
But what if he didn’t want to mirror it?
What if he wanted to replace it?

What if this isn’t a copy…
but a wound pretending to be a map?

— L.C.
